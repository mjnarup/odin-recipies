Pizza Recipe

This is a basic pizza recipe for the midnight munchies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Water
  • 12 Ounces of Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspon of black pepper
  • 3 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1/4 ounce of yeast
  • 1 1/3 cup of warm water
  • 1/3 cup of oil
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 6 ounces of pepperoni
